# Types of Hackers

### Hackers are not always bad, it actually depends on what they do and why they do it. there are different types of hackers based on their intentions and work.

## 1. White Hat Hackers
   These are ethical hackers. they work with permission and help organizations find security weaknesses. their main goal is to improve security and prevent attacks.

## 2. Black Hat Hackers
   These are the ones people usually think about. they hack without permission and do illegal activities like stealing data or damaging systems. their goal is mostly money or personal benefit.

## 3. Grey Hat Hackers
   They are in between white and black hat. they may hack without permission but not always with bad intentions. sometimes they report vulnerabilities, but still what they do is not completely legal.

## 4. Script Kiddies
   These are beginners who don’t have much technical knowledge. they use ready-made tools or scripts created by other hackers. they usually don’t understand the deeper concepts.

## 5. Hacktivists
   Hacktivists hack for social or political reasons. they target organizations or websites to spread their message or protest against something.

## 6. State-Sponsored Hackers
   These hackers work for governments. they are highly skilled and are used for cyber spying, intelligence gathering, or cyber warfare.

### so overall, hackers can be good or bad depending on their intention and how they use their skills.
