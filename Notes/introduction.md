# 🔐 Introduction to Cybersecurity

## 📌 What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is all about protecting systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, misuse, or attacks.

These days, so much of our life is online — banking, chatting, shopping, studying, storing files, and even working — so keeping digital things safe has become a big deal.

It is not only about stopping hackers. It is also about making sure data stays private, safe, and unchanged.

---

## 🌐 What is Cyberspace?

Cyberspace is the digital world created by connected computers, devices, networks, and the internet.

In simple words, it is the space where online activity happens — websites, apps, servers, games, communication, and everything else we use on the internet.

Whenever we go online, we are using cyberspace.

---

## ⚠️ What is Cybercrime?

Cybercrime is any illegal activity that involves computers, networks, or the internet.

It is usually done to steal information, damage systems, trick people, or misuse someone’s identity.

### Examples:
- Hacking into accounts
- Phishing attacks
- Identity theft
- Spreading malware

As people depend more and more on digital systems, cybercrime is also growing fast.

---

## 🧠 Important Terminologies

### 🔹 Vulnerability
A vulnerability is a weakness in a system, software, or network that an attacker can take advantage of.

### 🔹 Threat
A threat is anything that has the potential to cause harm to a system, network, or data.

### 🔹 Exploit
An exploit is a method or technique used to use a vulnerability in a system.

### 🔹 Malware
Malware means malicious software. It is made to damage, disrupt, or secretly control a system.

Examples include:
- Virus
- Worm
- Trojan

### 🔹 Firewall
A firewall is a security tool that monitors and controls incoming and outgoing network traffic based on rules.

### 🔹 Encryption
Encryption is the process of turning readable data into a secure form so that unauthorized people cannot understand it.

---

## 🎯 Why Cybersecurity Matters

Cybersecurity matters because almost everything today depends on digital systems.

If security is weak, data can be stolen, systems can be damaged, and personal or business information can be misused. That is why understanding the basics is so important.

---

## 📝 Summary

Cybersecurity is about protecting digital systems, networks, and information from attacks and misuse.

Some basic terms like vulnerability, threat, exploit, malware, firewall, and encryption are the foundation of this topic.

As technology keeps growing, understanding cybersecurity becomes more and more important.
